How to Use

Warduz Petshop Aquarium live monitoring system

Step 1: Setup your arduino water PH and temperature systems.

Step 2: Login to your webpage with Username and Password.

Step 3: After setting up the arduino hardware and logged in, you will be entering to the actual testing system website.

Step 4: Make sure that you enable your database.

Step 5: Start testing by clicking the start testing button.

Step 6: Wait for few seconds to measure your Water PH and Temperatures then results will popup after.

Step 7: Analyze if the water PH levels and Temperatures are safe to your freshwater/saltwater fish.
